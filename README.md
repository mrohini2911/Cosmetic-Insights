# 💄 Cosmetic Insights

An interactive Tableau dashboard developed to analyze cosmetic product data and provide meaningful business insights into brands, product categories, pricing, rankings, and skin-type suitability. This project helps cosmetic retailers, product managers, business analysts, marketing analysts, and e-commerce managers make informed business decisions through interactive visualizations and dashboards.

# 📖 Project Overview

The **Cosmetic Insights** project focuses on analyzing cosmetic product data using **Tableau Public**. The dashboard transforms raw cosmetic product information into meaningful visualizations that help users explore product distribution, compare brands, analyze pricing patterns, evaluate product rankings, and identify products suitable for different skin types.

The project follows a complete software development lifecycle (SDLC), covering every phase from ideation and requirement analysis to project design, development, testing, and deployment.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

The purpose of this project is to simplify cosmetic product analysis by presenting complex product data through an interactive Tableau dashboard. It enables users to identify trends, compare products and brands, and gain valuable business insights for better decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze cosmetic products across different categories.
- Compare cosmetic brands based on product availability.
- Analyze average product pricing by brand.
- Study the relationship between product price and ranking.
- Identify products suitable for different skin types.
- Display important KPIs using interactive KPI cards.
- Support business decision-making through interactive dashboards.
- Provide meaningful insights using Tableau Story.

---

# 💻 Dashboard Features

- Interactive KPI Cards
- Category Level Brand Distribution Analysis
- Category Level Product Distribution Analysis
- Brand-wise Average Pricing
- Top 10 Products by Product Count
- Top 10 Brands Suitable for All Skin Types
- Top 10 Brands and Cosmetics Count
- Category-wise Distribution of Combination Skin Products
- Category-wise Total Price Analysis
- Price vs Rank Scatter Plot
- Interactive Brand Filter
- Interactive Category Filter
- Tableau Story
